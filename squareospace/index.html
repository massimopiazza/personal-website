About Me

I am a 27-year-old engineer with 4+ years of working experience in the space industry and a MSc in Space Engineering from Politecnico di Milano.

My current role as a System Budgets Engineer is focused on establishing and monitoring technical budgets, by closely collaborating with performance engineers and contractors. I am also in charge of supporting the requirements management and change management processes, within the framework of a 600-satellite constellation program.

In my previous role, I was leading the development of advanced vision-based navigation techniques for close-proximity operations in space. This encompasses a broad variety of activities, ranging from the development of onboard software and project management to systems engineering and AIV/AIT support for a camera / star tracker flying on a small satellite mission.

My work on deep learning-based satellite navigation has been presented at international conferences and published in a peer-reviewed journal.

My academic background spans across a diverse array of disciplines, including: Systems Engineering, Deep Learning, Orbital Mechanics, ADCS, Space Propulsion, Signal Processing, Telecommunications and Structural Dynamics. Additionally, I have gained an early hands-on experience through my involvement with the Skyward Experimental Rocketry association.

While still in high school I embarked on an entrepreneurial journey, which began at the age of 17, as the CTO & Co-founder of WatchApp Inc. During this time I created innovative software keyboard solutions for iOS.
