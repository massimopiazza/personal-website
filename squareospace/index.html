About Me

I am a 27-year-old engineer with 4+ years of experience in the space industry and a MSc in Space Engineering from Politecnico di Milano.

My current role as a System Budgets Engineer is focused on technical budgets, requirements, and change management for a 600-satellite constellation program.

Previously, I led development of vision-based navigation techniques for close-proximity operations, covering onboard software, project management, systems engineering, and AIV/AIT support.

My work on deep learning-based satellite navigation has been presented at international conferences and published in a peer-reviewed journal.

My academic background spans Systems Engineering, Deep Learning, Orbital Mechanics, ADCS, Space Propulsion, Signal Processing, Telecommunications, and Structural Dynamics.

In high school, I co-founded WatchApp Inc., creating innovative software keyboard solutions for iOS.
